Biosketch

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the National Brain Research Centre (NBRC) in Gurgaon, where I work in the Cognitive Brain Dynamics Lab, investigating the dynamics of brain networks through the lens of nonlinear dynamics and computational modeling.

I obtained my B.Sc. with an Honors in Physics from St. Xavier’s College Kolkata, after which I completed my M.Sc. in Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras. Subsequently, I pursued my doctoral research at IIT Madras, focusing on the theoretical and computational analysis of complex dynamical systems. For detailed information, see my [resume].
