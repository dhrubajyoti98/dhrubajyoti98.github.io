Research

Interests

  • Emergent phenomena in dynamical systems: Synchronization, aging transitions and oscillation quenching, applications to computational neuroscience and quantitative biology etc.
  • High-performance computing: Applications of parallel computing using OpenMP, OpenACC, etc in scientific simulations.
