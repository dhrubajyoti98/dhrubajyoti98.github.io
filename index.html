About

Hello! I am Dhrubajyoti Biswas, and I am a physicist by training. I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the National Brain Research Centre (NBRC) Gurgaon, under the supervision of Prof. Arpan Banerjee. Prior to this, I obtained my MSc and PhD at the Department of Physics, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, working on problems of complex dynamical systems.

I currently stay on campus at NBRC Gurgaon, while my native place is back in the town of Kalyani, West Bengal. Apart from my academic interests, I take an interest in learning different programming languages and photography. I also play cricket from time to time.
