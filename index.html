👋 About

Hello! I am Dhrubajyoti Biswas, currently a Ph.D research scholar at the Department of Physics, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, Chennai. I work under the guidance of Dr. Vaibhav Madhok and Dr. Sayan Gupta. My current research interests are in the area of complex networks and high-dimensional dynamical systems. I am also interested in applications of high performance and parallel computing techniques, using APIs like OpenMP, OpenACC etc., in physics simulations.

I currently stay on campus at IIT Madras in Tamiraparani Hostel, while my native place is back in the town of Kalyani, West Bengal. Apart from my academic interests, I take an interest in learning different programming languages and photography. I also play cricket from time to time.
