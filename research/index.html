Three body dynamics under gravity is one of the classical problems that led to the genesis of the field which we now call dynamical systems. Beautiful pictures and simulations like the one above (which shows examples of periodic solutions to the three-body problem) had me hooked to this subject. While I find every aspect of this subject intriguing, one has to focus on specific things to make meaningful contributions. Thus, currently, I am working on:

  • Emergent phenomena in dynamical systems

    1. Synchronization

    2. Amplitude and oscillation death

    3. ...

  • High-performance computing

    1. Usage of multicore CPUs and GPUs for dynamical simulations
